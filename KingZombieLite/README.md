A lightweight browser-based zombie survival game where you must fight your way through 15 levels of a zombie-infested building to defeat the King Zombie!

## üéÆ Game Overview

You start with just a baseball bat at the ground floor. Fight through increasingly difficult zombie hordes, upgrade your weapons in the elevator between levels, and survive to reach the penthouse where the King Zombie awaits!

## üïπÔ∏è How to Play

### Controls
- **WASD** - Move your character
- **Mouse** - Aim your weapon
- **Left Click** - Attack
- **E** - Switch between unlocked weapons

### Game Mechanics

1. **Starting Out**: You begin at Level 1 with a baseball bat and 5 hearts of health

2. **Combat**: 
   - Eliminate all zombies in each level to progress
   - Different zombie types have different behaviors (fast, tank, explosive)
   - Your weapon has a range indicator showing your attack arc

3. **Elevator/Upgrades**:
   - After clearing a level, you enter the elevator
   - New weapons unlock as you progress through levels
   - Choose and switch between unlocked weapons
   - You heal 1 heart between levels

4. **Weapons Available**:
   - **Baseball Bat** (Level 1) - Your starting melee weapon
   - **Small Gun** (Level 2) - Basic ranged weapon
   - **Katana** (Level 2) - Sharp melee weapon with good damage
   - **Shotgun** (Level 5) - Powerful close-range weapon
   - **Assault Rifle** (Level 8) - Fast firing ranged weapon
   - **Chainsaw** (Level 10) - Brutal melee weapon
   - **Rocket Launcher** (Level 13) - Explosive damage for the toughest zombies

5. **Zombie Types**:
   - **Normal (Green)** - Standard zombies
   - **Fast (Red)** - Quick but fragile
   - **Tank (Purple)** - Slow but tough with high damage
   - **Explosive (Yellow)** - Explodes on death, damaging nearby entities

6. **Health System**:
   - You have 5 hearts of health
   - Zombies deal damage when they touch you
   - Brief invincibility frames after taking damage
   - Game ends if you lose all hearts

7. **Victory Condition**:
   - Reach Level 15 and defeat the King Zombie boss
   - The King Zombie has much more health and deals heavy damage

## üí° Tips for Success

- Keep moving! Don't let zombies surround you
- Learn weapon ranges and switch strategically
- Use the invincibility frames after getting hit to reposition
- Watch out for explosive zombies - they can damage you even after death
- Save your best weapons for higher levels
- The King Zombie is tough - come prepared with your best weapon!
